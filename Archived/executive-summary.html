Orion

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Description of Opportunity

Data professionals currently face a significant barrier: AI chatbots often lack awareness of the specific structure and content of the large datasets being analyzed. Because these tools cannot "see" the data context, they frequently generate generic or irrelevant responses, which severely limits their utility in technical workflows. There is a clear need for a specialized tool that bridges the gap between raw data and AI reasoning.

Business Concept

Orion is a modern AI agent designed to intelligently and seamlessly inject data context into AI conversations. By making AI models "data-aware," Orion transforms standard chatbot capabilities into a powerful, effective tool specifically tailored for data professionals. Our mission is to provide the digital "pen and paper" that allows modern researchers to translate complex data into significant human knowledge.

Industry Overview

The landscape for data science tools includes direct competitors like Google Colab, Hex and Deepnote, as well as indirect competitors like VS Code and Cursor. While many of these tools have AI assistants, Orion differentiates itself through its deep focus on automated data context injection.

Target Market

Orion targets a specific demographic of data-driven individuals:

  • Demographics: Students, academic researchers, and professionals (aged 15–40) at small to medium-sized companies.
  • Key Roles: Data Scientists, Quantitative Analysts, Business Analysts, and Academic Researchers.
  • Geographic Focus: A strong initial focus on the Boston scientific community, including institutions like Babson and MIT.

Competitive Advantage

  • Intelligent Context Injection: Unlike generic tools, Orion is built specifically to solve the data-awareness pain point.
  • Iterative Development: We prioritize intensive client communication and active feedback to improve the product at "lightning speed".
  • Community Integration: By publishing white papers and engaging in specialized communities like Towards Data Science and Stack Overflow, we build high-trust authority within our niche.

Business Model and Economics

Orion operates on a subscription-based model with a usage-based safety net:

  • Subscription Tiers:
    • Pro: $20 per month.
    • Ultra: $100 per month.
  • Usage Mechanics: Users receive a monthly quota where the cost of tokens accounts for 90%–100% of the plan price.
  • Pay-as-you-go: If quotas are exceeded, users can enable usage-based billing at the model's market price plus a 15% markup.

Team and Offering

Orion is currently led by a solo founder seeking a CTO to join the founding team and looking for $100,000 USD in pre-seed capital to fund initial operations.

  • Current Stage: Business concept/Alpha phase.
  • Timeline:
    • Alpha MVP: Rolling out to selected users in March 2026.
    • Public Release: Scheduled for June 2026.
